# Vision

LLMs are powerful, but long-running projects lose context. The longer a project runs, the more
its reality spreads across notes, chats, decisions, execution logs, TODOs, risks, assumptions,
questions, and research trails.

GotSaeng OS compiles those scattered materials into structured context packs. The first product
is not a chatbot, dashboard, or autonomous agent. The first product is a local-first,
model-agnostic context compiler.

The name GotSaeng OS has two meanings. It references **갓생**, the Korean internet phrase for
an intentional and high-agency life. It also reinterprets the phrase as **Got 生**, meaning
reclaimed life.

The humane goal is to reclaim scattered life, not automate life away.

GotSaeng OS is ADHD-aware, not ADHD-limited. It is human-in-the-loop, context-first, and designed
for anyone trying to recover continuity across fragmented attention, unfinished work, and
long-running goals.
